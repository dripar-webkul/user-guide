### Integration 

Integration in UnoPim is crucial for ensuring that product data is consistent, accurate, and easily accessible across various systems and channels. UnoPim provides RESTful APIs with OAuth 2.0 authentication for seamless third-party integrations.

### Magic AI

Magic AI is the ultimate tool for professionals looking to enhance their efficiency in product information management. UnoPim integrates Magic AI with support for **10+ AI providers** including OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, Ollama, and Groq.

Key capabilities include:
- **Content Generation** — Automatically generate product descriptions, SEO content, and more
- **Custom Prompts** — Define specific instructions for AI content generation
- **System Prompts** — Configure AI behavior and tone across the entire system
- **Auto-Translation** — Automatically translate product data across all configured locales
- **Magic Image** — Generate product images from text descriptions using DALL-E
- **AI-Powered Search** — Semantic search with embedding similarity
- **Encrypted Credentials** — Secure storage for all API keys and provider credentials
